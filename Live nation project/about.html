About LiveNation

LiveNation connects music to the listener and delivers an audience to the artis.Bult on ove 15 years of tenacity and neo-grassroots marketing, our unique company offers its clients almost every conceivable service available in the business of music.

We are a worldwide organization, desinged to provide on-the-ground support in countries far and wide. With a history of being at the forefront of independent thining, our company is leading the charge of a new music model, on that places the artis at both the crative and financial center. Our team knows the value of long-term strategic planning, and puts the artistin the creative drivers seat.

Founded in March 2020 by Spencer Russell as DCT Agency and later morphing in September 2020 into LiveNation, it is the umbrella company for many of our services.

The company is based on a team effort ideology and keeping the artist and it's counterpart a close knit family awith an evergrowing open discussion as to what is best for the artist long term career.
